About Me

Sahil Sharma, currently in his second year pursuing a Bachelor's in Technology with a major in Computer Science and Engineering at SRM University, Andhra Pradesh, originates from the dynamic Bokaro Steel City in Jharkhand. Commencing his academic journey at Sree Ayyappa Public School, Sahil has cultivated a deep passion for programming, specializing as a frontend developer and enthusiastic problem solver. His joy lies in crafting visually appealing and seamlessly functional user interfaces, evident in various front-end development projects that have enriched his coding skills. Beyond programming languages, Sahil is multilingual, effortlessly navigating conversations in English, Hindi, Bhojpuri, and Maghi, connecting with people on diverse levels.

Not content to settle, Sahil is currently exploring the realms of backend development, driven not only by languages and frameworks but by a profound understanding of the core principles of computer science. In his tapestry of academia, programming, and personal growth, Sahil believes that the fusion of passion and education, determination and technology, opens up boundless possibilities. As he evolves and learns, Sahil eagerly anticipates the digital impact he will make in the expansive world of computer science.
